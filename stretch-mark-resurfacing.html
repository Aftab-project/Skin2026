About This Treatment

Stretch Mark Resurfacing with Fractional RF + Microneedling is an advanced corrective treatment designed to visibly improve the appearance of stretch marks by targeting both the surface texture and the deeper structural layers of the skin. This dual-action approach combines medical-grade microneedling with radiofrequency energy to stimulate collagen remodeling, smooth uneven texture, and reduce the visibility of stretch marks.

Stretch marks form when the skin is stretched rapidly, causing the collagen and elastin fibers to break down. This treatment works by creating controlled micro-channels in the skin and delivering focused RF energy deep into the dermal layers, triggering the body's natural healing response. Over time, this process rebuilds collagen, smooths the skin's surface, and helps blend stretch marks with the surrounding skin tone.

Whether your stretch marks are from pregnancy, weight changes, or growth spurts, this treatment offers a safe, non-surgical solution to restore smoother, more even-looking skin. With consistent sessions, you can visibly reduce texture irregularities and regain confidence in your skin.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Fractional RF + Microneedling

Is the treatment painful?

No, the Hydrofacial is painless and comfortable. Most clients describe it as a refreshing, cooling sensation with gentle suction. There's no pain or discomfort during the treatment.

How long does the treatment take?

The full treatment typically takes 45 minutes, including consultation and aftercare guidance. This allows for a relaxed, thorough treatment without rushing.

When will I see results?

Results are visible immediately after the treatment with skin appearing refreshed and glowing. Optimal results develop over 2-4 weeks as the skin continues to regenerate and improve with proper aftercare.

Is there any downtime?

There is no downtime. You can return to normal activities immediately. We recommend avoiding intense exercise and direct sunlight for 24 hours to maximize results.

How long do the results last?

Results typically last 4-6 weeks. Most clients book maintenance treatments every 4 weeks to sustain their glowing complexion. Results can be cumulative with regular treatments.
