How the Treatment Works

Step-by-step breakdown of your treatment journey

1. Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a thorough consultation where we understand your skin type, concerns, and desired outcomes. Our specialists assess your skin condition and recommend the best approach for optimal results.

Duration: 15-20 minutes

2. Pre-Treatment Preparation

Your skin is gently cleansed and prepared. We apply any necessary protective products or numbing agents (if required) to ensure your comfort throughout the treatment.

Duration: 5-10 minutes

3. Treatment Application

The specialist applies the treatment using specialized equipment or techniques. You may experience sensations such as warmth, gentle pulsing, or light suction depending on the treatment type.

Duration: 30-45 minutes (varies by treatment)

4. Aftercare & Follow-Up Guidance

We provide comprehensive aftercare instructions including skincare recommendations, sun protection guidelines, and any specific do's and don'ts to maximize your results.

  • Avoid direct sunlight for 24-48 hours
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare
  • Stay hydrated
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours

Follow-up: We'll check in with you after 24-48 hours

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about this treatment

Is the treatment painful?

No, the treatment is generally painless. Most clients describe it as comfortable and relaxing. Some may experience mild sensations such as gentle warmth or light pressure, but this is not uncomfortable.

How long does the treatment take?

The treatment typically takes 45-60 minutes including consultation and aftercare instructions. This may vary slightly depending on specific needs.

When will I see results?

Some results are visible immediately after the treatment, with skin appearing refreshed and glowing. However, optimal results typically develop over the next 2-4 weeks as the skin continues to regenerate and improve.

Is there any downtime?

This treatment requires no downtime. You can return to your normal activities immediately. We recommend avoiding intense exercise and direct sunlight for 24 hours to maximize results.

How long do the results last?

Results typically last 4-6 weeks. Most clients opt for maintenance treatments every 4-6 weeks to sustain their beautiful results. Some benefits may be cumulative with repeated treatments.

Is it suitable for all skin types?

Yes, this treatment is suitable for all skin types including sensitive skin. However, we always conduct a thorough consultation to ensure the best approach for your specific skin condition.
