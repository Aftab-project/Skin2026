Treatment Information

Are your treatments suitable for all skin types?

Most of our treatments are suitable for all skin types. However, some treatments may not be appropriate for very sensitive skin or certain skin conditions. We'll conduct a consultation before your first treatment to ensure suitability.

Will treatments hurt?

Most facial treatments are relaxing and pain-free. Treatments like microneedling and HIFU may cause mild discomfort, but we use numbing cream when necessary. Many clients describe HIFU as a tingling or prickling sensation.

Is there any downtime after treatments?

Most treatments have little to no downtime. You may experience mild redness for a few hours after facials or microneedling. HIFU treatments typically have no downtime at all. Specific aftercare instructions will be provided for each treatment.

When will I see results?

Results vary by treatment:

  • Facials: Immediate glow, full results within 24-48 hours
  • Microneedling & Peels: Progressive improvement over 2-4 weeks
  • HIFU: Initial results in 2-4 weeks, full results in 2-3 months
  • BB Glow: Immediate results, optimal after 3-4 sessions

How often should I have treatments?

Treatment frequency depends on the type:

  • Maintenance Facials: Every 4-6 weeks
  • Intensive Treatments: Series of 3-6 sessions, 2-4 weeks apart
  • HIFU: Once every 12-18 months for maintenance
  • Chemical Peels: Every 4-6 weeks depending on strength

Can I wear makeup after treatment?

For most facial treatments, we recommend waiting 6-12 hours before applying makeup. For microneedling, wait 24 hours. BB Glow provides a makeup-like finish, so additional makeup isn't necessary immediately after.

HIFU Treatments

What is HIFU?

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a non-invasive treatment that uses ultrasound energy to lift, tighten, and tone skin. It stimulates collagen production deep in the skin layers, providing a natural lifting effect without surgery.

Is HIFU safe?

Yes, HIFU is FDA-cleared and has been used safely for over 15 years. It's a non-invasive procedure with minimal side effects. However, it's not suitable for pregnant women or individuals with certain medical conditions.

How long do HIFU results last?

HIFU results can last 12-18 months. As the treatment stimulates your body's natural collagen production, results continue to improve for 2-3 months after treatment. Maintenance treatments can extend results further.

Am I too young/old for HIFU?

HIFU is most effective for people aged 30-60 with mild to moderate skin laxity. Younger clients may use it preventatively, while those over 60 can still benefit, though results may be less dramatic than with surgical options.

Can HIFU replace a facelift?

HIFU is often called a "non-surgical facelift" and can provide lifting and tightening effects. However, results are more subtle than surgical facelifts. It's an excellent option for those wanting improvement without surgery or downtime.

Preparation & Aftercare

How should I prepare for my treatment?

General preparation tips:

  • Arrive with clean skin (no makeup for facial treatments)
  • Avoid sun exposure 48 hours before
  • Stay hydrated
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before
  • Inform us of any medications or skin conditions

Specific instructions will be provided when you book.

What aftercare is required?

General aftercare includes:

  • Avoid sun exposure and use SPF 30+
  • Keep skin moisturised
  • Avoid harsh products for 48 hours
  • No strenuous exercise for 24 hours (for intensive treatments)
  • Stay hydrated

Detailed aftercare sheets are provided after each treatment.

What should I avoid after treatments?

For 24-48 hours after most treatments, avoid:

  • Direct sun exposure
  • Swimming pools and saunas
  • Harsh exfoliants or active ingredients
  • Heavy makeup (for facial treatments)
  • Touching or picking at treated areas

Safety & Contraindications

Who should not have these treatments?

Treatments may not be suitable if you:

  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have active infections or open wounds
  • Have certain skin conditions (eczema flare-ups, severe acne)
  • Have taken Roaccutane in the last 6 months
  • Have metal implants in treatment area (for HIFU)
  • Have a history of keloid scarring

Always disclose your full medical history during consultation.

Are your products safe and high-quality?

Absolutely! We use only professional-grade, clinically-tested products from reputable brands. All equipment is regularly maintained and sanitised according to strict hygiene protocols.

Do you conduct patch tests?

Patch tests are recommended for clients with sensitive skin or allergies, especially before chemical peels and tinting services. Please request a patch test at least 48 hours before your appointment.
