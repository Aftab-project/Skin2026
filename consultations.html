Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about consultations

Is the skin analysis consultation really free?

Yes, our initial skin analysis consultation is completely free. This allows us to understand your skin and discuss your goals without any financial commitment.

Can I apply my consultation fee towards treatment?

Absolutely! Consultation fees are fully redeemable against the cost of any treatments you book based on our recommendations.

How long does a consultation take?

Consultations typically take 20"“40 minutes, depending on the complexity of your concerns and the treatment plan needed.

What's included in the £50 in-clinic consultation?

The in-clinic consultation includes a thorough skin assessment, discussion of your goals, personalized treatment recommendations, pricing details, and a customized treatment timeline.

Is a phone consultation as effective as in-clinic?

Phone consultations are excellent for discussing your goals and treatment options. However, in-clinic consultations allow for hands-on skin assessment and are recommended for complex cases.

Do I need a consultation before every treatment?

Your initial consultation includes a comprehensive plan. Follow-up reviews are recommended every 6"“12 months to assess progress and adjust your plan if needed.
