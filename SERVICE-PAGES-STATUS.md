## üìù Content Writing Tips

For each treatment page, research and include:

1. **What is it?**
   - Technical explanation made simple
   - How it differs from similar treatments
   - Technology or method used

2. **What it does?**
   - Step-by-step process
   - Specific skin concerns it addresses
   - Expected outcomes

3. **Key Benefits** (always 6 items):
   - Use icons from Font Awesome
   - Keep concise (1 sentence each)
   - Focus on unique selling points

4. **Who is it suitable for?**
   - Minimum 8 bullet points
   - Include skin types and concerns
   - Add contraindications in note box

5. **What to expect** (4-step timeline):
   - Consultation
   - Preparation
   - Treatment
   - Aftercare
   - Include time for each step

6. **Aftercare** (4 grid items + list):
   - Sun protection (always)
   - Hydration advice
   - What to avoid
   - Product recommendations

7. **Frequency & Duration**:
   - Accurate treatment time
   - Realistic frequency recommendations
   - Course vs maintenance guidance
